What is a TDU?

A TDU is a Transmiision and Delivery Utility. It is the company that delivers electricity from the company that generates it to your home. If you aren't sure who your company is you can check here

How much power do I use?

Most utility companies display your average monthly Kwh usage on your account online. If you don't have a current provider your can find a calcualtor online to estimate.
