What is a TDU?

A TDU is a Transmission and Delivery Utility. It's the company that delivers electricity from the company that generates it to your home. Don't know your company? Check here.

How much power do I use?

Most utility companies display your average monthly kWh usage on your online account. If you don't have a current provider you can use an online calculator to estimate usage such as this one.
